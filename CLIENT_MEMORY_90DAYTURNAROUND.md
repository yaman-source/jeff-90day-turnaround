## ICP And Prospecting Focus

Primary ICP:

- Founder-led or owner-operated businesses
- Usually $2M to $25M revenue, but revenue is not asked on the public form
- Western Canada first, but online work can expand beyond that
- Team size often 10 to 150 employees
- Founder/CEO/President/Owner/COO/VP-level decision maker

Pain points:

- Founder is still the bottleneck
- Growth has stalled
- Operational chaos despite revenue
- Team lacks accountability
- Sales pipeline is unclear
- No repeatable systems
- Scaling would break the business
- Succession or next-generation handoff is unclear

Target company types Jeff specifically discussed:

- Regional trades companies scaling multi-city: HVAC, electrical, plumbing, $3M to $10M
- Franchisors stuck at 5 to 15 units
- Industrial/service contractors: millwrights, fabrication, field service
- B2B distributors trying to modernize
- Founder-led ecommerce brands that plateaued
- Construction/home-services franchisee groups
- Businesses considering franchising, licensing, acquisition, multi-unit, or geographic expansion
